Matt Berry spends most of his free time pursuing his passions in the sky and in the mountains. He is a climber, skiier, skydiver, and B.A.S.E. jumper. His passions have led him all over the world to countries such as Argentina, Peru, Ecuador, Thailand, Japan, Mexico, and Canada. Although primarily a recreational climber and jumper, he is an American Mountain Guide Association (AMGA) Single Pitch Instructor and United States Parachute Association (USPA) certified skydiving Coach.

Matt is currently employed as a Mechanical Engineer for Black Diamond Equipment. He is responsible for all developmental testing on climbing hardware projects. As Matt and his friends spend majority of their free time playing in the mountains, the job allows him to give back to the community and ensure that all personal protective equipment that him and his friends rely upon for their safety is of first class quality and will perform as expected in the field.
